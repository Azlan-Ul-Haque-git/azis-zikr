🌿 Our Mission

Azis Zikr was created with one simple purpose: to make the remembrance of Allah ﷻ as effortless and beautiful as possible. In a world full of distractions, we wanted to create a space that brings your heart back to what truly matters.

The app is completely free, contains no advertisements, tracks no personal data, and is built purely as an act of ibadah for the Ummah of the Prophet ﷺ.

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْهُ خَالِصًا لِوَجْهِكَ الْكَرِيمِ

O Allah, make this purely for Your noble Face.
